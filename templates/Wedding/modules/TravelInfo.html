Air Travel

San Jose Airport (SJC) is 15 minutes (10 miles) from Cupertino, CA. Recommended airport for domestic travel. Note: Southwest airlines has 2 free checkin in bags.
San Francisco Airport (SFO) is 40 minutes (33 miles) from Cupertino, CA.

    Ground Transportation.

    If you don't have a rental car, Caltrain will take you anywhere between San Francisco and San Jose. Uber and Lyft is available almost anywhere in the Bay Area.

    Within SF, you can take Uber, Lyft, MUNI, BART, Cable car, Street Car and Buses

    In the South Bay, you mainly eat Asian food, shop at Great Mall (outlet) or Valley Fair Mall & Santana Row, go hiking or biking in Rancho San Antonio, and go drive around Tech companies (Google, Apple etc...).
