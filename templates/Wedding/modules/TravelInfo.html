Things to Do... Things to See...

Silicon Valley

In the South Bay, you mainly eat Asian food, shop at Great Mall (outlet) or Valley Fair Mall & Santana Row, go hiking or biking in Rancho San Antonio, and go drive around Tech companies (Google, Apple etc...). You can't go into the companies unless you know someone that works there. It is home to Levi's Football Stadium.

San Franciso

  • Twin Peaks - one of best views of SF
  • Conzelman Rd, Marin County - one of best views of Golden Gate Bridge
