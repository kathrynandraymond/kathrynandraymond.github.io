Air Travel / Transportation

Air Travel

San Jose Airport (SJC) is 15 minutes (10 miles) from Cupertino, CA. Recommended airport for domestic travel.

San Francisco Airport (SFO) is 40 minutes (33 miles) from Cupertino, CA.

Ground Transportation

If you don't have a rental car, Caltrain will take you anywhere between San Francisco and San Jose.

Uber and Lyft is available almost anywhere in the Bay Area.

Within SF, you can also take BART or MUNI/Bus/Cable Car
